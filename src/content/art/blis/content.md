Blis wants to know how she became... different. Why did crystal horns sprout from her head? Why do cracks in her skin glimmer when she's nervous? Why do strands of metal like tin and bismuth grow from her head like hair? Was she born this way, or is it somehow tied to her mother's death?

Is Blis somehow becoming... a pokémon?

Yet again I drew a character for a tabletop roleplaying game! Blis is part of a high fantasy pokemon world. Born in a far north rural village, her family herded snogoats. Or rather, her father did, as her mother passed away really early in life. Since then, Blis started exhibiting... pokémon-like powers, metallic powers specifically.

In search of answers, Blis left her hometown with her <small>(clumsy)</small> snogoat partner, Tintin. Maybe if she can understand pokémon, she'll come to understand herself.
